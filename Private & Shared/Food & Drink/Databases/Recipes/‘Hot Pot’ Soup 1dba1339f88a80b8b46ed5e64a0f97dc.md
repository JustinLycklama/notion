# ‘Hot Pot’ Soup

Status: New
Type: Food

Stovetop for 3 mins

- Oil
- Sliced onion
- White bits (minus bottom) of green onioN

Add 3-4 cloves of garlic

1 big spoonful of korean spice pepper

2 big spoonfuls of miso paste (soy tub from fridge)

After 1 min, add water (1 bowl per person) + dashi powder

---

Add all the veggies and random things!

- Cabbage
- Spinach (Last 30 seconds)
- Mushrooms
- Fish balls
- Dumplings
- Tofu (medium firm)
- 

Top with green onion heads from first half
